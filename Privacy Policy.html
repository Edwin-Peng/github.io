Privacy Policy / 隱私政策

1. Data Collection Scope

We only collect publicly available data from social platforms (such as public posts, comment statistics, and engagement metrics). We do not collect any data from private accounts, closed groups, or non-public content.

2. Purpose of Data Use

The collected data is used solely for academic research and statistical analysis. It will not be used for any commercial purpose, advertising, or user profiling.

3. Data Retention and Sharing

The research data is only accessible to the research team. No personally identifiable raw data will be shared externally. Only aggregated and anonymized results may be published. Data will be deleted after the research project concludes, following institutional guidelines.

1. 資料收集範圍

3. 資料保存與分享

研究資料僅限研究團隊內部使用，不會公開可識別個人身分的原始資料。發表成果僅以彙總、匿名化的統計結果呈現。研究結束後，資料將依照學術機構規範刪除。
